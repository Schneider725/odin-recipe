oatmeal

beyond simple. beyond fiber

ingredients

  • 80 grams oat bran
  • 240 grams milk
  • 240 grams water
  • 20 grams brown sugar
  • 20 grams maple syrup
  • teaspoon cinnamon
  • pinch of salt

instructions

  1. bring the milk and the water to a boil
  2. add oats and salt
  3. after the oats thicken, add brown sugar, maple syrup and cinnamon
