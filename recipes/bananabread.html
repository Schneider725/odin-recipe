banana bread

mmmm banana bread

ingredient

  • flour
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • eggs
  • bananas

instructions

  1. combine dry ingredients in bowl
  2. beat the butter and the eggs. mash the bananas with this mixture
  3. combine the wet and the dry
  4. pour into pan loaf and bake at 350 for 60 minutes
